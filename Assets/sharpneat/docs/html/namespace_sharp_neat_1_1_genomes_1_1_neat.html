◆ CorrelationItemType

enum SharpNeat.Genomes.Neat.CorrelationItemType
strong

An enumeration of connection gene correlation types. Mismatched genes can be disjoint or excess. This distinction is defined by original NEAT but is probably redundant for most genome comparison/distance metrics.

Enumerator
Match 

A match between two connections in two distinct genomes.

Disjoint 

A connection with no match in the other genome (that we are comparing with) and that has an innovation ID less than the highest innovation ID in the other genome,

Excess 

A connection with no match in the other genome (that we are comparing with) and that has an innovation ID higher than the highest innovation ID in the other genome.
