\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The prey capture task\textquotesingle{}s grid world, as defined in\+: 

Incremental Evolution Of Complex General Behavior, Faustino Gomez and Risto Miikkulainen (1997) \href{http://nn.cs.utexas.edu/downloads/papers/gomez.adaptive-behavior.pdf}{\texttt{ http\+://nn.\+cs.\+utexas.\+edu/downloads/papers/gomez.\+adaptive-\/behavior.\+pdf}}

Encapsulates the agent\textquotesingle{}s sensor and motor hardware, and the prey\textquotesingle{}s simple stochastic movement.

The grid world\textquotesingle{}s origin, i.\+e. coordinate (0,0), is at the bottom left of the grid.
