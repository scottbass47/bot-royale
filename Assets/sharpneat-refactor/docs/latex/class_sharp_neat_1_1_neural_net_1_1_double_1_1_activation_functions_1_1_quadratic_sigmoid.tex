\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A sigmoid formed by two sub-\/sections of the y=x$^\wedge$2 curve. 

The extremes are implemented as per the leaky \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sharp_neat_1_1_neural_net_1_1_double_1_1_activation_functions_1_1_re_l_u}{Re\+LU}}, i.\+e. there is a linear slop to ensure there is at least a gradient to follow at the extremes.
